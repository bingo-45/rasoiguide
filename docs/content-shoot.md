# Dal Tadka stage photography plan

The canonical manifest names eleven required frames. Shoot every frame in the same steel cooker or pan, on the same terracotta cloth, from the same 42-degree camera angle with soft window light from camera-left. Lock white balance and focal length. Do not use stock or generated food images.

Capture: washed dal water level, sealed cooker, dropped pressure pin, whisked consistency, cumin crackle, pale-gold garlic, tadka pour, finished bowl, plus paired recovery states for watery dal, burnt cumin, and over-browned garlic. Export an 800 px WebP and a 1400 px WebP for each stage, keep the original raw files outside the app repository, and update the photography manifest from `planned` to `ready` only after a human content review.
